Our Story
Every Story has a beginning, but ours has a beautiful beginning.
Amber Shines Jewellery began in 1995 with a thought to change the
way imitation jewellery was seen & with the idea to make the brand a
synonym to fashion jewellery.


                   We grew into a renowned house at the forefront of innovative
                   jewelry design with expert craftsmanship.
